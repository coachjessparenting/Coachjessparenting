The problem isn't you

If you've been swinging the same hammer at every parenting problem, that's a toolbox problem, not a you problem.

Maybe that hammer is yelling. Maybe it's gentle redirection that gets ignored every single time. Maybe it's timeouts, or counting to three, or bribing with screen time just to get through the afternoon. And sometimes it works, a little, sometimes, in certain situations. But you know in your gut it doesn't work for everything. Because it was never designed to.

Positive Discipline gives you a full toolkit, not one strategy for every moment, but the ability to read what's actually happening and reach for exactly the right tool. Tantrums, defiance, sibling fights, power struggles, big emotions. Each one has its own anatomy. This course teaches you to see it.

"If you want a more robust, complete toolbox, and to know the exact tool, when to reach for it, and why it works. This course was built for you."
